<!-- ── 04 Three processors ── -->

<section class="section" style="background-color: #f9f9f9;">
  <div class="container">
    <p class="is-size-7 has-text-grey has-text-weight-bold mb-2">/ 04</p>
    <h2 class="is-size-3">Three processors, one open platform</h2>
    <p class="is-size-5 mt-4 mb-6">The tbd-16 runs three purpose-built processors in parallel. Each layer is independently programmable — change one without touching the others.</p>
    <div class="columns">
      <div class="column">
        <h3 class="is-size-5 has-text-weight-bold">ESP32-P4</h3>
        <p class="is-size-6 has-text-grey mb-2">DSP Engine</p>
        <p class="is-size-5">Dual RISC-V @ 400 MHz with 32 MB PSRAM. Handles all real-time audio DSP, USB 2.0 OTG, and AI/vector instructions.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="column">
        <h3 class="is-size-5 has-text-weight-bold">RP2350B</h3>
        <p class="is-size-6 has-text-grey mb-2">UI / MIDI</p>
        <p class="is-size-5">Dual Arm Cortex-M33 @ 150 MHz with 520 KB SRAM, 12 PIO state machines. Drives the hardware UI, MIDI, sequencers, and arpeggiators.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="column">
        <h3 class="is-size-5 has-text-weight-bold">ESP32-C6</h3>
        <p class="is-size-6 has-text-grey mb-2">WiFi</p>
        <p class="is-size-5">WiFi 6 (802.11ax) and Bluetooth 5. Manages the web interface, preset management, and Ableton Link.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

        <p class="is-size-5 mt-4">All TRS connections follow the <a href="https://minimidi.world/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">TRS Type-A standard</a> — no adapters or special cables needed. Connect keyboards, sequencers, controllers, or Eurorack gear via clock/reset input.</p>
        <p class="is-size-5 mt-4">USB MIDI works class-compliant (no drivers on any OS). The USB-A host port lets you plug in MIDI controllers directly. For wireless tempo sync, use Ableton Link instead of MIDI clock.</p>
