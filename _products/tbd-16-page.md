      <h2 class="is-size-3">Meet the TBD-16</h2>
      <p class="is-size-5">TBD-16 is a 16-channel open-source DSP toolkit — a standalone desktop instrument for sound design, experimentation, and live performance. It brings together more than 50 high-quality generators and effects within a modular, easily extensible architecture.</p>
      <p class="is-size-5 mt-4">Designed to welcome users beyond the Eurorack community, the TBD-16 includes standard MIDI connectivity, a redesigned intuitive web user interface, and UX guidelines to help developers build playable, musician-centered DSP modules.</p>
      <p class="is-size-5 mt-4">By uniting developer flexibility with musician usability, TBD aims to offer a resilient, open-source alternative in a landscape dominated by proprietary platforms. All software is released under GPL 3.0, and updated open hardware designs will be published in KiCad.</p>
      <p class="is-size-5 mt-5">Learn more and contribute on <a href="https://github.com/ctag-fh-kiel/ctag-tbd" target="_blank" rel="noopener">GitHub</a>.</p>
