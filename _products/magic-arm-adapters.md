Magic Arm Adapters are 3D-printed parts that make mounting solenoid beaters onto industry-standard magic arms simple and secure. Ideal for experimenting with unconventional setups — at home, in the studio, or anywhere you go searching for sounds.

### What's in the box

- 6 × Magic Arm Adapter (3D-printed)
- Compatible with standard magic arm clamps

### Open design

As always, dadamachines embraces openness and user creativity. The adapter's STL design is available for download, allowing you to remix, modify, or print it yourself.
