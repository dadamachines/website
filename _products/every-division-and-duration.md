        <h1 class="title is-2 mb-5">every division and duration</h1>
        <p class="is-size-5">Every Division and Duration is a polytemporal instrument in 12HP Eurorack format.</p>
        <p class="is-size-5">Play with six different, musically connected tempi.</p>
        <p class="is-size-5">Create tuplets in realtime via grid MIDI controller.</p>
        <p class="is-size-5">Time itself as an expressive musical parameter.</p>
