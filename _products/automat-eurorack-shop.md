The automat eurorack brings mechanical music directly into modular synthesizer setups. Patch any trigger or gate sequencer into the module and play physical objects — cans, boxes, drums, metal sheets, or any surface that makes a sound. 8HP, 6 trigger/gate inputs, 6 universal DC outputs.

**Two versions available:**
- **Module only** — includes high-quality power supply — **349 € + VAT**
- **Full kit** — module + power supply + 6 solenoid beaters + cables + LED splitters — **699 € + VAT**

**USB Host** — Front-panel USB Type-A for direct Launchpad connection. No computer required. In standalone mode, the module runs sequencing firmware and becomes a fully standalone mechanical instrument.

**Potentiometer extension (4HP)** — optional companion module with 6 dedicated knobs, one per output. Each adjusts trigger length for expressive control over how hard or soft the solenoid strikes.
