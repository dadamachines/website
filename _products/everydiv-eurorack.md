The everydiv eurorack is a playable polytemporal eurorack instrument. 6 independent clock outputs, each with configurable division/duration tuplet pairs (1–32), producing polyrhythms, polymeters, and tempo modulations that stay musically connected.

Played via a Novation Launchpad over USB. Internal tempo (40–300 BPM), MIDI clock input, and analog clock input. Three sync modes, four reset modes, full MIDI CC control. Settings stored on microSD as human-readable JSON.

Developed with <a href="https://baem.studio/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Nicolas Bougaïeff</a> (BAEM / NovaMute) and Jelle Akkerman.
