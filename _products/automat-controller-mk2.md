The automat controller MK2 is the standalone controller board — the same brain as the [automat toolkit MK2](/shop/automat-mk2/), without the solenoids, mounting hardware, and power supply. Ideal if you already own actuators and accessories, or want to upgrade an existing setup.

### What's included

- 1 × automat controller MK2 (RP2350-based)
- 1 × USB-A to USB-C cable

### All MK2 features built in

**USB Host mode** — Plug a Novation Launchpad directly into the controller's USB-A port. No computer needed. Supports Launchpad S, Mini (Mk1–3), Mk2, X, Pro, and Pro Mk3.

**Built-in sequencer** — 12 channels × 8 steps with ratchets, accents, skip steps, per-channel length and direction, trig conditions, tap tempo, and MIDI clock sync.

**Backward compatible** — In Device mode, the MK2 works exactly like the original automat controller. Same [WebUI configurator](https://config.dadamachines.com/), same learn modes.
