LED Splitters add a bright LED indicator to every solenoid beater. For the first time, audiences can see both the movement and a clear visual signal for each trigger. No soldering or electronics expertise required — just plug and go.

### What's in the box

- 12 × LED Splitter cables
- Compatible with all dadamachines solenoids

### How it works

Each LED Splitter sits between the automat controller output and the solenoid. When a trigger fires, the LED lights up in sync with the beater strike. Instant visual feedback for performances, installations, and studio sessions.

Works with [automat controller](/shop/automat-controller-mk2/), [automat toolkit](/shop/automat-mk2/), and [automat eurorack](/shop/automat-eurorack/).
