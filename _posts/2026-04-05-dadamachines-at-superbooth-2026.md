We're coming back to **Superbooth** — May 7–9 at FEZ Berlin — and we're bringing five new products.

### What we're showing

- **[tbd-16](/products/tbd-16/)** — A 16-channel open-source DSP toolkit. 50+ plugins, three processors, sub-millisecond latency. A complete desktop instrument that boots straight into music — no computer required.

- **[tbd-core](/products/tbd-core/)** — The same board without the box. For instrument designers and product developers who want to build custom hardware around the TBD platform.

- **[everydiv eurorack](/products/everydiv/)** — A polytemporal eurorack instrument. 6 independent clocks with division/duration tuplet pairs, played on a Launchpad. Polyrhythms, tempo modulations, and rhythmic territory no single clock can reach.

- **automat toolkit MK2** — The next generation of the automat. Same plug & play philosophy, new capabilities.

- **automat eurorack** — Mechanical music inside your modular system. An 8HP eurorack module with 6 trigger/gate inputs, 6 DC outputs, and USB Host for standalone Launchpad sequencing.
